Foreman OS

On-Site AI Infrastructure

Your AI. Your building. Your data.

Foreman OS is our flagship product: a proprietary AI operating system that runs on hardware we deploy inside your business. It does what cloud AI services do — automation, decision support, document intelligence, operations workflows — without any of your data ever leaving the building it was generated in.

For businesses that handle sensitive contracts, proprietary methods, customer records, or regulated information, renting your AI infrastructure from a cloud provider is a liability. Your prompts go to someone else’s server. Your outputs train someone else’s models. Your operations depend on uptime you don’t control. Foreman OS removes all of that. The models run on your hardware, the data stays on your network, the workflows are built to your business.

You own the deployment. We design, install, and maintain the system — but the infrastructure, the models, and the data are yours. No subscription lock-in, no vendor dependency, no silent updates that change how your AI behaves. If we walked away tomorrow, your Foreman OS would still be running the same way.

This is AI infrastructure you own, not AI you rent.

OneForty

Longevity & Health

Live to 100. Feel like 40.

OneForty is an AI-native health-performance platform built for people who want to add healthy decades to their life — not just hit a step goal. It unifies the data streams that usually live in five different apps (training, sleep, readiness, nutrition, supplements, lab biomarkers, body measurements) into a single intelligence layer that produces one prescription each morning: what to eat, how hard to train, what to take, and what to recover from.

Behind the scenes, OneForty pulls sleep, HRV, training load, nutrition, and biomarker data into one intelligence layer, detects hidden patterns, and turns it all into plain-English coaching. Workouts come with rep-by-rep AI-narrated audio in whatever coaching style fits you that day — from drill sergeant to zen. Macros get estimated from photos. Meal plans and grocery lists generate weekly. Lab results become readable health reports. Progress gets visible and celebrated. The complexity stays hidden; the clarity shows up.

The mission is simple and the headline says it on every screen: Live to 100. Feel like 40.

Innermost

Human Orientation

The next best step starts within.

Innermost is an AI-guided human orientation and life-design platform. Where most self-help tools push inspiration, Innermost does the opposite — it triages. You can’t talk purpose to someone whose foundation is crumbling, so the app assesses stability across six domains (physical, emotional, financial, social, purpose, habits), maps them onto a five-layer pyramid (safety → stability → belonging → confidence → direction), and surfaces the weakest layer as priority before touching anything aspirational.

The core loop — Assess → Stabilize → Orient → Activate — drives every feature. A structured intake captures values, strengths, frustrations, stability, and aspirations. Claude analyzes the full profile to generate a personalized MTP (Massive Transformative Purpose), a hard truth, a poison to remove, a strength to lean into, and a phased roadmap. Ongoing discovery conversations and journaling progressively deepen the profile over time — the MTP isn’t a one-shot quiz result, it evolves. Periodic check-ins track stability shifts; life events are logged; the roadmap adjusts.

Built on a specific worldview: triage before transcendence, minimal necessary intervention, remove poison before adding nutrition, balance over ideology, harmony with reality. The philosophy isn’t decoration — it’s embedded in every AI prompt as a non-optional doctrine, and critical triage decisions (priority layer, framework phase, foundation-at-risk flag) are computed deterministically server-side so the AI can’t drift into generic advice.

Pudwiser

Baking Intelligence

Perfect puddings. Wherever you roast.

Pudwiser is premium baking intelligence for Yorkshire puddings. Elevation, humidity, and air pressure change how batter rises — and fan ovens, cupcake tins, and cast iron all behave differently. Pudwiser takes one classic, trusted base recipe and calibrates it to your exact location and kitchen setup, so you get the same confident rise every time.

Save your home recipe, troubleshoot a bake that didn’t quite work, and share results with a community of bakers posting their weekly rises. No self-raising flour, no guesswork, no random recipes from the internet — just one recipe, dialled in for you.

  • Location-aware — adjusts for elevation, humidity, and pressure
  • Kitchen-tuned — fan or conventional, cupcake tin or cast iron
  • Trusted, not random — one classic recipe, calibrated every time
  • Community — weekly featured bakes from other users
  • Troubleshooter — diagnose a flat or soggy bake
