The Quote Machine

AI-powered quoting for construction contractors. Upload a spec PDF, get a detailed itemized quote in minutes.

AI for Construction

Most general contractors still build quotes the way they did 30 years ago: print the drawings, spread them on a desk, and tally materials and labour by hand. A moderately complex residential bid takes a day; a commercial spec package can burn a week of billable time. Then the client emails back a tweak, and the whole process restarts.

The Quote Machine replaces that loop. A contractor uploads an architect's spec PDF — up to 300 pages, including drawings — and a multi-pass AI engine classifies the document by CSI division, extracts every material and labour line, and produces a fully formatted quote (PDF + Excel breakdown) in under 10 minutes. The analyzer is section-aware: schedules are parsed as structured data, drawings are read with GPT-4o vision for dimensions, cover pages are ignored. Contractors get per-pass visibility, one-click retries on sections that missed, and token-level cost transparency.

The client-facing side is just as considered. Every quote generates a unique, sanitized portal link — internal pricing, markups, and overhead never leave the server. Clients can accept with signature, decline with a reason, or request specific changes via a free-text message. Those requests surface on the contractor's dashboard with one-click “Create Revision” buttons. Revisions are versioned with a side-by-side diff view so both sides see exactly what moved between v1 and v2 — items added (green), removed (red), modified (blue) with dollar deltas.

Other touches: automated follow-ups for quotes that were viewed but not responded to, expiry-aware nudges before validity dates lapse, a clients directory tying every quote to a customer record, saveable templates from any past quote, and a time-savings dashboard that shows how much time is being reclaimed each month vs. manual quoting.
