Products

Built to prove a point.

Every product here solves a real problem for real people. They also prove how we think, build, and execute. If you like what you see, we can build something similar for you.

140

AI-guided personal development. Helping people identify their drivers and take the next best step.

Personal Development

140 is built on the idea that personal growth starts with self-awareness. The app uses AI to guide users through a structured process of identifying their core drivers, values, and blind spots. It then builds a personalized roadmap — not generic advice, but specific next steps based on who you actually are. Think of it as a personal strategist that listens more than it talks. Whether you're navigating a career change, building better habits, or just trying to figure out what matters most, 140 meets you where you are and moves you forward.

Pudwise

Nutrition intelligence that meets people where they are. Practical, personalized, no gimmicks.

Health & Nutrition

Pudwise takes the guesswork out of eating well. Instead of rigid meal plans or calorie counting, it uses AI to understand your actual eating patterns, preferences, and goals — then gives you practical, personalized guidance you can act on today. It's built for real people with real lives: busy schedules, picky eaters in the house, and budgets that matter. Pudwise doesn't judge what you ate yesterday. It helps you make a better choice right now. Nutrition intelligence that works with your life, not against it.

The Quote Machine

Business automation that runs while you sleep. Quoting, workflows, and operations on autopilot.

Business Operations

The Quote Machine is what happens when you stop tolerating admin friction and start automating it. It handles quoting, invoicing, client follow-ups, scheduling, and operational workflows — all running in the background while you focus on actual work. Built for small and mid-size businesses that are drowning in process but can't afford a full ops team. The Quote Machine doesn't replace your people. It removes the repetitive tasks that burn them out. Less clicking, less chasing, less "I forgot to send that." More building, more selling, more living.

Innermost

An AI-guided orientation platform. Helping people identify their deepest drivers and take the next best step.

Coming Soon

Innermost goes deeper than 140. It's a full orientation platform designed to help people understand the forces shaping their decisions, relationships, and sense of purpose. Using guided AI conversations and structured reflection, Innermost surfaces the beliefs and patterns running in the background — the ones most people never examine. It's not therapy. It's not coaching. It's a mirror with better questions. Currently in development and coming soon.

About

Grounded in reality.

I've spent my career in high-stakes environments where clarity matters and the wrong call has real consequences. That background taught me one thing above all else: deal with what matters most, first. Everything else can wait.

That mindset is the foundation of this company. I help people and organizations understand what's real, figure out what matters, and use the right tools to solve the real problems.

AI is powerful. But it's a tool. A lever, not a replacement. The people holding it are what matter. That's why every engagement starts with the human problem, not the technology.

Whether I'm on stage, in a boardroom, or building a custom system, the question is always the same: what's the real problem, and what's the next best step?
